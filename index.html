Published his second novel "El Filibusterismo" in Ghent, Belgium, continuing his critique of colonial society.

Returned to the Philippines and founded La Liga Filipina, a civic organization aimed at social reforms.

1896

Martyrdom

Executed by firing squad on December 30, 1896, at Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park), becoming a martyr for Philippine independence.
